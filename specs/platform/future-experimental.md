# Future-Forward and Experimental Systems Specification

## Overview

This specification covers future technologies, experimental features, prefigurative politics, community defense, communications independence, and speculative design. These systems enable communities to experiment with emerging technologies, build autonomous infrastructure, and practice the future we're building.

## Advanced Future Technologies

### REQ-FUTURE-001: Drone and Aerial Coordination
The platform SHALL coordinate use of delivery drones and aerial systems for community logistics.

**Rationale**: Autonomous drones can deliver medicine, food, supplies while reducing vehicle traffic; community ownership ensures democratic control.

#### Scenario: Medical supply drone delivery
- **GIVEN** community operates delivery drone network
- **WHEN** urgent medical supplies needed
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Dispatch appropriate drone for payload
  - Coordinate flight paths safely
  - Navigate to delivery location autonomously
  - Track delivery completion
  - Build aerial logistics capacity
  - Reduce vehicle dependency

### REQ-FUTURE-002: Agricultural Monitoring Drones
The platform SHALL coordinate agricultural drones for crop monitoring and analysis.

**Rationale**: Aerial monitoring enables precision agriculture; early detection of problems enables targeted response.
#### Scenario: Crop health monitoring
- **GIVEN** community farms need monitoring
- **WHEN** using agricultural drones
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Schedule flights over growing areas
  - Capture multispectral imagery
  - Analyze crop health and water stress
  - Identify pest or disease early
  - Optimize irrigation and intervention
  - Build precision agriculture capacity
