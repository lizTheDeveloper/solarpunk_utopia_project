# Housing and Built Environment Specification

## Overview

This specification covers alternative housing models, natural building techniques, public space transformation, and creating built environments that support community, ecology, and human flourishing. These systems enable affordable housing, sustainable construction, and transformation of public space into commons.

## Alternative Housing Models

### REQ-HOUSING-001: Co-Housing Community Formation
The platform SHALL support formation and operation of co-housing communities with shared spaces and private dwellings.

**Rationale**: Co-housing combines privacy with community, reduces resource use through sharing, and creates built environments for mutual support.

#### Scenario: New co-housing development
- **GIVEN** members want to form co-housing community
- **WHEN** organizing co-housing project
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Coordinate founding member group formation
  - Support participatory design process
  - Share co-housing models and best practices
  - Coordinate financing and legal structure
  - Connect with experienced co-housing communities
  - Build from vision through occupancy

#### Scenario: Existing co-housing operations
- **GIVEN** a co-housing community is established
- **WHEN** managing daily operations
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Coordinate common house scheduling
  - Manage shared meals and cooking rotation
  - Coordinate maintenance of shared spaces
  - Support decision-making processes
  - Track shared resource usage
  - Build community cohesion

### REQ-HOUSING-002: Land Trust Coordination

#### Scenario: Community land trust formation

## Natural Building

### REQ-HOUSING-006: Natural Building Workshops
The platform SHALL organize natural building workshops teaching cob, earthbag, straw bale, and other low-impact construction.

**Rationale**: Natural building uses local, non-toxic, low-carbon materials; sharing knowledge makes sustainable construction accessible.

#### Scenario: Cob building workshop
- **GIVEN** community wants to learn cob construction
- **WHEN** workshop is organized
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Find experienced cob builders to teach
  - Coordinate hands-on building project
  - Teach material sourcing (clay, sand, straw)
  - Practice building techniques
  - Address building codes and regulations
  - Build natural building capacity

### REQ-HOUSING-007: Natural Building Material Sourcing
The platform SHALL coordinate sourcing of natural building materials including clay, straw, stone, and timber.

**Rationale**: Natural building uses local, non-toxic, low-carbon materials; sharing knowledge makes sustainable construction accessible.
#### Scenario: Material acquisition for natural build
- **GIVEN** a natural building project needs materials
- **WHEN** sourcing locally
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Identify local clay deposits (with landowner permission)
  - Coordinate straw bale acquisition from local farms
  - Source local stone and timber sustainably
  - Share material preparation knowledge
  - Build supply networks for natural building
  - Reduce embodied energy in construction

### REQ-HOUSING-008: Green Roofs and Living Walls
The platform SHALL coordinate installation and maintenance of green roofs and living walls.

**Rationale**: Green infrastructure provides cooling, stormwater management, and habitat; shared skills enable widespread adoption.
#### Scenario: Community green roof project
- **GIVEN** a building can support green roof
- **WHEN** installation is planned
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Assess structural capacity
  - Design appropriate green roof system
  - Coordinate installation work party
  - Establish plant community
  - Organize ongoing maintenance
  - Track stormwater, cooling, and biodiversity benefits

### REQ-HOUSING-009: Reclaimed Building Materials
The platform SHALL facilitate exchange of reclaimed and salvaged building materials.

**Rationale**: Demolition creates waste; salvage creates resources and preserves embodied energy.

#### Scenario: Deconstruction material redistribution
- **GIVEN** a building is being deconstructed
- **WHEN** materials are salvaged
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Coordinate careful deconstruction
  - Catalog salvaged materials (lumber, fixtures, doors, windows)
  - Match with building projects needing materials
  - Store materials if needed
  - Reduce construction waste and costs
  - Build circular construction economy

### REQ-HOUSING-010: Owner-Builder Support Networks
The platform SHALL create support networks for owner-builders undertaking their own construction.

**Rationale**: Self-building reduces costs dramatically; peer networks make it accessible to more people.
#### Scenario: Owner-builder project support
- **GIVEN** a member undertakes self-construction

## Building Codes and Policy

### REQ-HOUSING-023: Building Code Reform Advocacy
The platform SHALL coordinate advocacy for building codes that allow natural building, tiny houses, and alternative construction.

**Rationale**: Overly restrictive codes prevent sustainable, affordable housing; collective advocacy can reform regulations.

#### Scenario: Tiny house code advocacy
- **GIVEN** building codes prohibit tiny houses
- **WHEN** community advocates for reform
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Research model codes and precedents
  - Coordinate testimony at public hearings
  - Build coalition with advocates
  - Propose specific code amendments
  - Pressure decision-makers
  - Legalize housing alternatives

### REQ-HOUSING-024: Occupancy and Zoning Reform
The platform SHALL advocate for zoning reforms that enable density, co-housing, and diverse housing types.

**Rationale**: Exclusionary zoning prevents density and diversity; reform enables affordable housing types.
#### Scenario: Missing middle housing advocacy
- **GIVEN** zoning only allows single-family or large apartments
- **WHEN** advocating for reform
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Push for legalization of duplexes, triplexes, courtyard housing
  - Advocate for ADU allowances
  - Support co-housing zoning provisions
  - Build coalition for housing diversity
  - Enable affordable housing types
  - Fight exclusionary zoning
