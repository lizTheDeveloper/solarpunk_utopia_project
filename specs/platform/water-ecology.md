# Water and Ecology Systems Specification

## Overview

This specification covers water commons, ecosystem restoration, biodiversity, waste reduction, and circular economy practices. These systems support regenerative relationships with ecosystems, water sovereignty, and transition to zero-waste circular economy.

## Water Commons and Conservation

### REQ-WATER-001: Rainwater Harvesting Network
The platform SHALL coordinate rainwater harvesting systems across the community for water resilience and conservation.

**Rationale**: Every roof is a watershed; capturing rainwater reduces storm water runoff, recharges groundwater, and builds water independence.

#### Scenario: Community rainwater infrastructure
- **GIVEN** members install rainwater catchment
- **WHEN** systems are connected
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Map rainwater catchment capacity across community
  - Coordinate storage tank sharing or bulk purchases
  - Share installation and filtration knowledge
  - Track gallons harvested community-wide
  - Integrate with irrigation and greywater systems
  - Celebrate water independence milestones

#### Scenario: Rainfall-responsive irrigation
- **GIVEN** community tracks rainfall and stored water
- **WHEN** irrigation is needed
- **THEN** the system SHALL:
  - Coordinate irrigation from rainwater reserves
  - Defer municipal water use when reserves adequate
  - Optimize water use across gardens
  - Share water during drought from those with surplus
  - Build community water resilience

### REQ-WATER-002: Greywater Reuse Systems
