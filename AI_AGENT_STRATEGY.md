## Privacy-Preserving Design

### How AI Learns Without Surveillance

1. **Local-First Processing**: Sensitive data never leaves device
2. **Federated Learning**: Agents learn from patterns, not individual data
3. **Aggregation Only**: "20 people need plumbing help" not "Alice has clogged drain"
4. **User Control**: Opt-in to all learning; transparency about what's learned
5. **Differential Privacy**: Mathematical guarantees against individual identification
6. **Community Auditing**: AI algorithms are open source and community-auditable

### What AI Never Does

‚ùå Sell data to third parties
‚ùå Profile individuals for manipulation
‚ùå Make decisions without human input
‚ùå Optimize for engagement/addiction
‚ùå Operate as surveillance system
‚ùå Serve corporate interests over community

## Democratic AI Governance

### Community Control Mechanisms

1. **Priority Setting**: Community votes on what AI capabilities to develop
2. **Performance Oversight**: Regular review of AI agent effectiveness
3. **Algorithm Audits**: Community can inspect and modify AI behavior
4. **Override Rights**: Humans always override AI suggestions
5. **Opt-Out Options**: Every AI feature is optional
6. **Collective Training**: Community shapes AI through feedback

### Anti-Bias Practices

- **Diverse Training Data**: Include marginalized voices and perspectives
- **Bias Auditing**: Regular testing for demographic discrimination
- **Equitable Access**: AI serves all community members equally
- **Cultural Competence**: AI respects diverse cultures and traditions
- **Disability Justice**: AI centers disabled users in design

## Technical Implementation Notes

### Model Architecture

- **Open Source Models**: Llama, Mistral, or similar for local deployment
- **Fine-Tuning**: Community-specific models trained on local patterns (with consent)
- **Multi-Modal**: Text, voice, image understanding for accessibility
- **Edge Computing**: Run on community servers or distributed devices
- **Federated**: Coordinate across devices without central server

### Integration Points

- **Natural Language Interface**: Conversational interaction primary UI
- **Structured Data**: AI reads/writes to platform databases
- **Event System**: Agents subscribe to platform events, act proactively
- **Message Queue**: Asynchronous agent coordination
- **Knowledge Graph**: Shared understanding of community resources and relationships

### Resource Requirements

- **Old Phone Friendly**: Lightweight models that run on minimal hardware
- **Bandwidth Efficient**: Designed for low connectivity, offline-first
- **Battery Conscious**: Minimize compute to preserve battery on solar charging
- **Graceful Degradation**: Core features work even if AI unavailable
